    FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


    Is your mask medical-grade?
    Yes. Our medical masks are certified by the FDA & NHS

    How many sizes do you offer?
    One Size. All designs are printed on a 7” x 3.5” doubly ply face mask with elastic earloops

    What are the wash instructions?
    Machine Wash Cold, Gentle Cycle. Tumble Dry Low. Do Not Iron. Do Not Bleach

    Does the MightyMask mask prevent getting COVID-19?
    Our medical masks will sharply mitigate the risk if used properly
    However, fashion masks do not come with guarantee as these are not PPE

    Why aren’t you custom printing N95 masks
    N95 are designed for aerosol-generating procedures and not for
    casual use by the public, as requested by public health advisors

    Are there any other benefits to wearing a mask?
    Facial coverings are used to protect not only you, but those around you
    An asymptomatic and contagious individual increases transmission risks.
    Wearing a mask is one simple way to help reduce that risk.

    What is your return policy?
    We are unable to accommodate refunds or cancellations unless product at fault

    What type of filter fits in the mask? Where can they be purchased?
    Any standard medical grade filter fits. Purchase online or with a local supplier

    If I place an order, will you donate a mask every order that I place?
    Absolutely! We donate one mask for every mask purchased through MightyMask
